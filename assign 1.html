My Favorite Hobby: Photography

Why I Love Photography

Photography is more than just a hobby to me—it's a way of capturing memories and freezing moments in time. It allows me to express my creativity and see the world from unique angles.

I started taking pictures with my phone, and soon I was hooked. The feeling of clicking a perfect shot is unmatched.
Now I carry my DSLR wherever I go, always ready to capture nature, people, and candid emotions.

What I Use

I currently use a Canon EOS 1500D and a tripod for stable shots. I also enjoy editing my photos using Lightroom to enhance lighting and color balance.

Biography: A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Early Life

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, born in 1931 in Rameswaram, India, came from a humble background. He worked hard and studied physics and aerospace engineering. His early life was filled with curiosity and determination.

Achievements

He was the 11th President of India and is often referred to as the "Missile Man of India" for his work in the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology. He played a key role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.

Legacy

Dr. Kalam passed away in 2015 while delivering a lecture. He remains an inspiration to students and scientists across the globe. His books like "Wings of Fire" continue to motivate young minds.

My Daily Routine

Morning

I usually wake up at 6:30 AM. I start my day with a quick stretch and some meditation to refresh my mind. After that, I take a shower and enjoy a light breakfast while reading the news.
By 8:00 AM, I begin my studies or work tasks for the day.

Afternoon

After a productive morning, I take a lunch break around 1:00 PM. I try to eat healthy—rice, dal, and vegetables are my go-to. Then I usually relax for a bit, maybe read a book or take a short nap, before getting back to more work or attending online classes.

Evening

The evening is for winding down. I go for a walk or sometimes click photographs during sunset. After returning home, I help with household chores or watch a documentary. By 10:30 PM, I call it a day and go to bed.
